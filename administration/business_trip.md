# Business Trip (Conference)

## Pre-Conference Checklist

- **Registration**  
  - Try to register as soon as possible. Registration may be sold out before the official deadline due to limited capacity.

- **Book Flight**  
  - Use [CTP](https://us.ctmsmart.com) for booking.  
  - If it does not work, reach out to Kim.

- **Reserve Accommodation**  
  - Referring to the recommendation on the conference homepage may help.

- **Request I-20 (for international travel)**  
  - Submit your I-20 request via [M-Passport](https://mpassport.umich.edu/).  
  - Processing typically takes **1â€“2 days**.  
  - Be sure to print the signed I-20 document and carry it with you for re-entry into the U.S.

- **Submit Reimbursement Request**  
  - Include all eligible expenses such as **Transportation**, **Accommodation**, and **Meals**.
